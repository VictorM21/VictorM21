## 🧠 Interview Prep Topics

### Technical
- [ ] RAG architecture (chunking strategies, embedding models, reranking)
- [ ] LangChain LCEL chains and runnable composition
- [ ] Vector database trade-offs (FAISS vs Pinecone vs Weaviate vs Chroma)
- [ ] Claude API: messages format, system prompts, tool use, streaming
- [ ] FastAPI: dependency injection, async endpoints, pydantic models
- [ ] Docker: multi-stage builds, volumes, networking
- [ ] ML fundamentals: bias/variance, cross-validation, regularization

### Behavioral (STAR format)
- [ ] "Tell me about a time you built something end-to-end"
- [ ] "How do you handle ambiguous requirements?"
- [ ] "Describe a data project that had business impact"
- [ ] "How do you stay current with AI developments?"

### Questions to Ask Interviewers
- "What does the AI engineering stack look like today?"
- "How do you evaluate LLM output quality in production?"
- "What's the ratio of building vs. maintaining existing systems?"
- "How does the team stay current with the pace of AI change?"
